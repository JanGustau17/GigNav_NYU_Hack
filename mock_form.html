Delivery Worker Minimum Pay Rate Complaint

Use this form to report potential violations of NYC's minimum pay rate for app-based delivery workers. The current minimum rate is $22.13/hr effective April 1, 2026.

Important: Under NYC Administrative Code 20-1522, delivery apps must pay workers at least the minimum pay rate for all hours worked, including on-call time. Filing this complaint is protected activity - your delivery app cannot retaliate against you.
